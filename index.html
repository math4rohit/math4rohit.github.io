About Me

I am a Cryptography Analyst at QNu Labs, Bangalore, working on Post-Quantum Cryptography, Fully Homomorphic Encryption, Secure Multi-Party Computation, and Zero-Knowledge Proofs, with applications to secure outsourced computation and privacy-preserving healthcare analytics.
